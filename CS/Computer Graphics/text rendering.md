

Text rendering spans multiple important Computer Science domains.

# Main Areas

|CS Area|Why Text Rendering Belongs Here|
|---|---|
|Computer Graphics|Rendering vectors, pixels, anti-aliasing, GPU drawing|
|Operating Systems|Font engines, display systems, rendering pipelines|
|Typography & Digital Fonts|Font design, hinting, glyph shaping|
|Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)|Readability, perception, UX|
|Graphics Programming|Rasterization, shaders, texture atlases|
|Rendering Engines|Browser/UI rendering systems|
|Computational Geometry|Bézier curves, vector mathematics|
|Image Processing|Anti-aliasing, filtering, subpixel rendering|
|GPU Systems|Hardware acceleration, compositing|
